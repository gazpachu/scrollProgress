scrollProgress

by Jeremias Menichelli

Small library that creates a progress bar that indicates how much you've scrolled on a website. It's very useful to show the reading progress in an article or a blog post.

Scroll down and see how it works or take a look at the official documentation.

Download the code from the repository or download it from bower and npm as scrollprogress.

Support: Chrome, Firefox, Safari and IE9+
